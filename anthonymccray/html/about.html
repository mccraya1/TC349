About Anthony

I am rising print and web designer. I am a graduating senior at Michigan State University, studying Media and Information with a specialization in interactive and social media. My heart was set on design at a very young age. I've always loved drawing, solving problems, and experimenting with computer programs. It was only natural for me to study graphic design in my college career.

In my college years I've learned what it means to be a true designer. Designing is more than just creating an aesthetically pleasing graphic. True design is designing with the intent to solve a probleem. Whether it is inventing a new household product, making a new iPhone feature, or building a website for a client, designers should work as though they are problemer solvers.
